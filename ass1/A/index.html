About PICT

Pune Institute of Computer Technology (PICT) is a leading engineering college established in 1983. We are committed to providing quality education in engineering and technology fields, fostering innovation and research excellence.

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry collaborations ensure that students receive comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers in technology and engineering.
